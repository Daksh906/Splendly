Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the information you provide directly: your email address and password when you register, and the expense records you create (amount, category, date, and description). We do not collect payment information or any data beyond what is needed to run the service.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — displaying your expenses, generating summaries, and allowing you to manage your records. We do not use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than operating the app for you.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, but no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell or share your personal data with third parties. We may use infrastructure providers (such as hosting services) to run the app, but these providers are bound by confidentiality obligations and do not have independent access to your data.

5. Your Rights

You may access, update, or delete your account and associated data at any time. If you delete your account, your expense records are permanently removed from our systems. You may also request a copy of your data by contacting us.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the app's support channel.
